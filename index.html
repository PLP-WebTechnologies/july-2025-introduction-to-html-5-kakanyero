Published on july 31, 2025

Water Conservation Techniques

Learn how to reduce water usage while keeping your plants healthy:

  • Install a rain barrel to collect rainwater
  • Use drip irrigation systems
  • Mulch around plants to retain moisture

Composting Basics

Turn your kitchen scraps into nutrient-rich soil:

  1. Choose a compost bin location
  2. Layer green and brown materials
  3. Turn the pile regularly
